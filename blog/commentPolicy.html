Comment Policy

This is just a few rules to ensure the discussions in the comments don't go off track.

  • Don't be a jerk.
  • Don't use slurs. (Includes comments with embedded OneJoke™ technology)
  • Try to be constructive.
  • Try to include at least three numbers or one symbol in your comment.
  • Don't promote your stuff unless it's on-topic and helpful.

If you violate any of these rules, I'll probably delete your comment -- or worse, ban you! Oh no.
